About This Service

This is a free port scanning service for Yggdrasil Network users. We automatically detect your IPv6 address and scan it for open ports, helping you identify potential security vulnerabilities. No installation required - just click scan!

Quick Scan: Scans 1000 most common ports (30-60 seconds)
Full Scan: Scans all 65,535 ports (5-15 minutes)

Supports both 200::/8 (node addresses) and 300::/8 (subnet addresses)
Rate limits: Quick scan once per 30 seconds, Full scan once per 60 seconds
